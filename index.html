Sub-Saharan Africa context

Leading causes of maternal mortality in SSA: haemorrhage (25%), hypertensive disorders (16%), sepsis (11%), unsafe abortion (8%), obstructed labour (6%).

MgSO₄ is first-line for eclampsia prophylaxis and treatment. Misoprostol for PPH prevention where oxytocin is unavailable.

Malaria in pregnancy increases risk of anaemia, low birth weight, and maternal mortality — treat per national protocol.
